# Procedure: Apple Watch Reset and Transfer

**Category:** Client device management  
**Applies to:** Transferring an Apple Watch to a new owner (family member, resale, decommission)

## When to Use

- Giving a watch to a family member
- Selling or recycling an Apple Watch
- Removing a device from your Apple Account inventory

## Steps

### 1. Unpair and Erase (preferred — removes Activation Lock)

1. Keep Apple Watch and iPhone **close together**
2. Open **Watch app** on iPhone → **My Watch** tab
3. Tap **All Watches** (top left)
4. Tap **Info (i)** next to the watch
5. Tap **Unpair Apple Watch**
6. For cellular models: choose to remove cellular plan
7. Enter **Apple Account password** to disable Activation Lock
8. Tap **Unpair** to confirm

iPhone automatically creates a backup before erasing.

### 2. Remote Removal (if watch is already with new owner)

1. Go to [iCloud.com/find](https://www.icloud.com/find)
2. Sign in with your Apple Account
3. **Find My** → **All Devices** → select Apple Watch
4. Click **Erase Apple Watch**
5. Click **(X)** or **Remove from Account** once erased

### 3. Preparation

- Clean with non-abrasive, lint-free cloth (casing + sensors)
- Include magnetic charging cable (older watches need specific chargers)

### 4. New Owner Setup

New owner holds watch near their iPhone to begin standard setup.

If new owner doesn't have an iPhone: use [Family Setup](https://support.apple.com/en-us/109015) to manage from your iPhone.

## Verification

- Watch no longer appears in your Apple Account devices list
- New owner can complete setup without Activation Lock prompt
- Your data is not visible on the device
